RoR2 Build Planner

Click items to add them to your build.
Click items in the selection boxes to remove them.
Drag items to reorder them in each rarity category.
Use the filters to find the items you need.
Overlay function on the bottom right corner allows you to see your build in-game. Tap items below to add or remove them from your build.
