RoR2 Build Planner

Click items below to add them to your build.
Click items inside the selection boxes to remove them.
You can drag items to reorder them in each rarity category.
Use the filters to find the items you need.
Overlay function on the bottom right corner allows you to see your build in-game using Picture-in-Picture mode. Resize the "Selected Items" section to adjust the aspect ratio of the overlay.
